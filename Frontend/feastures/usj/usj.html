Admissions Dates & Deadlines

December/January Intake

File Submission: From January 7 to February 6

Result Announcement: First week of April

Final Confirmation Deadline: May 30

June Intake

File Submission: From June 1 to June 30

Result Announcement: First week of August

Final Confirmation Deadline: September 4

Required Documents

  • Two passport-sized photos.
  • A copy of the Lebanese ID or passport.
  • A certified copy of the Lebanese Baccalaureate or its equivalent.
  • Official transcripts for the last three academic years.
  • A copy of the National Social Security Fund (NSSF) card (if applicable).
  • Registration card for the French Proficiency Test.

French Language Proficiency

Applicants must meet the following French proficiency requirements:

Tuition Fees

Tuition fees vary per academic track and are determined per credit at the beginning of each academic year.

Fees are paid in two installments per semester. A deposit may be required before first-time registration.

Refunds are not available if the student withdraws.

For more details, visit www.usj.edu.lb
