Late Admission for Spring 2025-2026

Deadline: July 15, 2025

Required Documents

  • A recent passport-size photo.
  • A clear photocopy of your identity card or passport.
  • Family Civil Status record.
  • Official transcript from schools you attended in the last three years..
  • Certified copy of official diplomas when available.
  • SAT scores (optional).
