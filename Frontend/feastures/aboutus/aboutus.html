About Us

Path2Uni

We are a team of five passionate individuals, united by our commitment to helping students navigate the university application process. Path2Uni is a project created for our CMPS 271 course, where we aim to provide valuable resources and support for students pursuing higher education. Our diverse backgrounds and skills allow us to offer a comprehensive platform filled with expert advice, useful tools, and personalized guidance. Together, we strive to make the journey to university smoother and more accessible for all students, helping them unlock opportunities for success.
