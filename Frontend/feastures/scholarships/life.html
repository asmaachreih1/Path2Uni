Scholarship Details

LIFE Scholarship

partial scholarship

Benefits

Tuition fees, mentorship, networking, and career support.

Universities Covered

Selected universities in Lebanon for undergraduate applicants (or abroad for graduate applicants).

Majors Covered

Economics, Finance, Engineering, Computer Science, Corporate Law, or Mathematics.

Requirements

Lebanese students with financial need, academic excellence, and drive for leadership.

Application Details

Online application, including essays and financial documents.

Required documents include academic transcripts, proof of financial need, and writing essays.

Deadlines

Varies yearly. Check the official website.
