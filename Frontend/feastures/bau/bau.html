First Admissions Test

Test Date: Starting Saturday, March 16th

Submission Opens: Monday, December 10th

Deadline: Wednesday, March 13th

Second Admissions Test

Test Date: Starting Monday, July 15th

Submission Opens: Monday, May 06th

Deadline: Friday, July 12th

Required Documents

  • Photocopy of Lebanese Secondary School Certificate or registration in Grade 12.
  • Official high school transcripts (Grades 10 & 11) in a sealed envelope.
  • SAT I scores (if applicable).
  • Photocopy of Civil Status Record (Lebanese applicants only).
  • Photocopy of ID or passport (non-Lebanese applicants only).
  • Two recent passport-sized photos.
  • Non-refundable application fee: L.L. 200,000
